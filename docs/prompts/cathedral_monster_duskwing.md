## Prompt

Painterly Elysian-Cathedral illustration, square 1:1 format, gothic arch frame with leaded stone pillars and trefoil and quatrefoil cornices in the spandrels, plain leaded inner border (regular enemy). Subject: the Duskwing — a swarm of five to seven small winged shadow-flitters wheeling in formation across a large pale-silver full moon centered in the arch. Each flitter is a clean dark silhouette of a leaf-shaped membranous wing-pair with a small tucked body, no facial detail, pure shape, backlit by the moon. The gilding motif marks the swarm: each flitter's wing-edges and body-seam are traced with a thin hairline of gold-gilt that catches the moonlight, and a faint amber inner glow shows at the core of each silhouette like a caged ember carried aloft — two tiny amber eye-pinpoints on each. The lead flitter is largest in the foreground, gilt-edges brightest, the rest receding into the moon. Background: a dominating stained-glass moon disc, deep-indigo night-sky panels around it, a faint distant spire silhouette below the moon, thick dark leading lines separating segments, painterly tone variation within each pane. Colors permitted: pale-silver moonlight, deep-indigo night, dark wing-silhouette, hairline gold-gilt wing-edge, warm amber inner-glow and eye-pinpoints, warm-gold trim, dark-leading-outline. Dramatic and atmospheric, NOT scary. No fangs, no blood, no close-up faces. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel itself filling the square canvas.
