## Prompt

Stained glass landscape panel, 21:9 panoramic format, Elysian Cathedral card-art style: a Sylvanwood glade with THE GREAT THORNKIN TREE as the dominant central feature. SIGNATURE FEATURE — the Great Thornkin Tree: a colossal ancient tree towering over all other trees in the forest, occupying the center of the composition and rising the full height of the canvas, with a massive gnarled broad trunk and sprawling canopy. The trunk is patterned with deep bark furrows and ancient moss-green patina; crucially, the Great Thornkin Tree has a LARGE CARVED FACE in its bark — closed peaceful wise eyes-like knots, a thick bearded-root mustache formed by hanging roots and vines, and a broad benevolent mouth-like hollow at the base that looks like a cavernous entrance opening into darkness. The face reads as ancient and wise, clearly intentional not accidental — a sentinel tree-spirit. Surrounding the Great Thornkin Tree: a dense canopy of smaller ancient forest trees fading into the background haze on left and right; shafts of warm golden sunlight filtering down between the trunks in diagonal rays from the upper left. Foreground: dappled moss-covered forest floor with ferns and a few tiny twinkling pink-and-gold wisp-lights drifting in the air (small sparkle motes, suggesting sprites without rendering them as creatures). Deep emerald-green dominant palette with jade mid-tones, warm amber sun-shaft highlights, and the Great Thornkin Tree's rich brown bark + moss-green trunk. Thick dark leading lines separating glass segments, painterly stained-glass tone variation within each segment. Horizontal composition — the Great Thornkin Tree is the central anchor, the forest is the surround. Colors permitted: deep-emerald-green, jade-green, forest-green, moss-green, warm-brown-bark, rich-walnut-trunk, ancient-patina-green, warm-amber-sunshaft, dappled-gold-light, pale-wisp-sparkle-pink, soft-ivory-highlight, dark-leading-outline.
