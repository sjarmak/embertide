## Prompt

Stained glass cathedral window illustration, square 1:1 format, in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style: gothic pointed arch frame with leaded sandstone pillars left and right entwined with dusty-amber desert vines and tiny glass-panel sun-bleached leaves, trefoil quatrefoil cornices in the arch spandrels with lapis-lazuli inlay borders, weathered spiral glyphs carved into the keystone above (wild-boss treatment — extra ornamental flourish, denser leading than a regular). Subject: the Iron Sentinel — a colossal vault-sentinel automaton standing centered in the arch, broader than it is tall, filling about sixty-five percent of the arch height with hugely intimidating bulk. It is a hollow suit of ancient sandstone-and-bronze siege-armor animated by the caged Embertide's overflow: a squat fortress of a body, no living flesh inside, every plate pitted and wind-scoured. Build it from first principles — two great slab-like shoulder buttresses flaring wide from the torso, each capped with a domed bronze boss; a blocky drum-shaped torso bound by riveted iron bands; thick pillar-legs planted in a wide, immovable stance like temple columns. Where a head would sit there is only a low bronze cowl with a single narrow horizontal vent-slit, lightless except for two faint amber pinpoints deep inside. Its arms are heavy counterweighted mauls of solid stone-and-metal — no separate weapon, the fists themselves are the bludgeons — one drawn back and raised in a slow, clearly telegraphed wind-up so a child reads "a big hit is coming." Across every surface runs our gilding motif: a creeping network of glowing gold fracture-lines branching through the seams and rivet-rows like cracks spreading through old glaze, a faint warm amber light leaking out from the joints as though the Embertide is burning hollow inside the shell, thin wisps of pale-gold vapor curling from the shoulder-vents. Background: stained-glass panels in warm sandstone-tan, terracotta, and lapis-blue arranged as radial cream sun-rays bursting outward behind the figure, terracotta dune-ridges along the lower band, a lapis sky-band across the top, thick dark leading lines separating segments, painterly tone variation within each pane, faint amber rim-light thrown by the gilding glow. Warm sun-washed cathedral lighting from upper left on the stone arch frame only. Colors permitted: weathered-sandstone-plate, dusk-bronze-armor, deep-iron-shadow, cracked-gold fracture-lines, warm-amber inner-glow, amber-vent-glint, pale-gold vapor, terracotta-dune, lapis-lazuli inlay, sandstone-tan background, dusk-rose haze, tarnished-gold rivet, dark-leading-outline. No face, no living features, no banners, no heraldry — a brutal hollow sentinel silhouette. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel filling the square canvas.
