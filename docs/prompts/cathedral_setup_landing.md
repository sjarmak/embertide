## Prompt

Painterly Elysian-Cathedral stained-glass illustration, landscape 16:9 cinematic format, in the style and coloring of the Gilded Cage region backdrop: sapphire-blue dominant with gold ornament accents, warm sun-washed highlights from the upper left, thick dark leading lines separating glass segments, painterly stained-glass tone variation within each segment, reverent and ancient atmosphere, the architecture is the subject.

The scene: a single vast cathedral hall seen from inside. Tall gothic pointed arches in cream-pale marble with gold-leaf capitals rise on the left and right edges, framing the composition like a triptych. Stone pillars in cool sapphire-blue shadow. The lower two-thirds of the frame is the empty cathedral nave — polished marble floor, soft dust-mote haze, cool sapphire air — quiet, reverent, anticipatory.

In the upper third of the frame, set high in the rear cathedral wall, a single CIRCULAR ROSE WINDOW. The window's tracery is architecturally ornate: a stone-carved circular ring with intricate trefoil and quatrefoil cornices around its outer edge, dark wrought-iron leading dividing the radial petals, gold-leaf filigree along the rim. Architectural beauty only — clean, restrained tracery without scattered symbols or repeating motifs around it.

The rose window's glass is rendered as a single SUNSET GRADIENT that flows softly across the entire window: coral-pink at the upper edge melting into warm amber-gold through the center and peach-rose at the lower edge, with faint pearl-white highlights catching the light. The gradient is one continuous painterly wash; the leading lines divide the petals architecturally but the color flows through them as a unified sunset glow.

At the rose window's exact center: the EMBERTIDE itself — a single radiant off-center WHORL of molten gold, a luminous spiral of living ember-light coiling outward like a captured aurora drawn into a coil, outlined and traced with thick dark leading along its turning arms. The Embertide whorl is the rose window's only emblem; nothing else surrounds it. Its glowing heart is filled with translucent pearlescent rose-gold opal glass that blazes warmly with the same sunset tones, the molten-gold spiral arms fading from brilliant white-gold at the core to deep amber at their tips.

Warm amber-gold light shafts beam down from the rose window through dust motes onto the marble floor, fading to soft sapphire haze in the lower portion of the frame. The lower 70% of the image is empty cathedral atmosphere ready for UI to sit on top.
