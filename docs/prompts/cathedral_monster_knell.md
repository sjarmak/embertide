## Prompt

Stained glass cathedral window illustration, square 1:1 format, in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style: gothic pointed arch frame with leaded stone pillars left and right cast in saturated royal-purple shadow-stone, trefoil quatrefoil cornices in the arch spandrels, an ornate rose-window motif in the upper arch with a gold-leaf rose and an interlocking gold-filigree ring of concentric sound-wave scrolls (region-boss treatment — densest composition, heaviest leading). Subject: the Knell — a tolling bell-spirit hovering at the center of the arch, filling about sixty-five percent of the arch height, dramatic and predatory. Build it from first principles: its entire body IS a single enormous cracked bronze bell, mouth-down, suspended in midair with no clapper-yoke and no floor beneath it — a hulking cast-bronze bell-shell, its surface webbed with deep fracture-cracks as though it has been struck once too hard and the toll never stopped. There is no face and no separate head; instead a single glowing resonance-glyph is etched dead-center on the bell's broad face, a circular sigil radiating a low magenta light, the only "eye" of the creature. From beneath the bell's rim hang TWO long heavy chain-arms — articulated bronze links trailing down and outward, each ending in a blunt striker-maul like a great bell-hammer, the two mauls raised and drawn back symmetrically in a clearly telegraphed double-strike wind-up so a child reads "a loud hit is coming." Across the whole body runs our gilding motif intensified for a boss: glowing gold fracture-lines flood every crack in the bronze, pouring warm amber-gold light out from inside the hollow bell as though the caged Embertide is ringing trapped within it, thin streamers of pale-gold vapor rising from the bell's crown, and the gilt-cracks pulse brightest around the resonance-glyph. Concentric magenta sound-ripple rings emanate outward from the bell-face into the panes. Background: a stained-glass rose pattern of rhythmic concentric sound-wave rings, deep-indigo and royal-violet panels with silver-moonlight accents in the outer rim and a warm-magenta resonance glow blooming behind the bell, a flat stained-glass backdrop only — no interior architecture, no receding hallway, no floor-tile perspective. Thick dark leading lines separating segments, painterly tone variation within each pane. The viewer's first impression should read as a glowing cracked bronze bell ringing in violet midnight. Colors permitted: cracked-bronze bell-shell, deep-bronze shadow, cracked-gold fracture-lines, warm-amber inner-glow, pale-gold vapor, magenta resonance-glyph glow, royal-violet, deep-indigo background, silver-moonlight accent, magenta sound-pulse, bronze chain-link, dark-leading-outline. No face, no hands, no eye-orb, no drum, no drumsticks, no held tools — the body is a bell and its arms are chains. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays, no inscriptions of any kind — just the stained-glass panel filling the square canvas.
