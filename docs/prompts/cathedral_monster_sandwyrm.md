## Prompt

Stained glass cathedral window illustration, square 1:1 format, in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style: gothic pointed arch frame with leaded sandstone pillars left and right entwined with dusty-amber desert vines and tiny glass-panel sun-bleached leaves, trefoil quatrefoil cornices in the arch spandrels with small lapis-lazuli inlay accents, plain leaded inner border (regular-enemy treatment). Subject: the Sandwyrm — a hostile burrowing desert predator centered in the arch at about sixty-five percent of arch height, its armored head bursting upright from a violent sand-eruption in a mid-strike rearing pose. The head is a long cream-and-sandstone armored skull, predatory and serpentine, jaws agape across the front to show rows of jagged ivory fangs lining both upper and lower jaws — a bony fang-cage circling a dark throat. A thick prehensile feeding-tongue coils outward through the open jaws like a striking ribbon. The eyes are small recessed pits, hollow and shadowed at the sides of the skull, never round friendly beads. The segmented trunk curves behind the head in stacked plated cream-and-terracotta armor rings with rough ridges along the spine, the lower coils still buried in the sand-burst. Our gilding motif corrodes the armor: hairline gold fracture-lines branch across the cream plates with a faint warm-amber glow leaking from the seams, brightest where the plates meet, as though the creature is being slowly gilded from within. A violent dust-cloud of cascading sand-grains and small rock fragments explodes outward from the burrow-point at the base. Background: warm sandstone-tan panels with dusk-rose haze rays angling down, terracotta-orange dune ridges along the lower band, a lapis-lazuli sky band along the upper edge, thick dark leading lines separating each segment, painterly tone variation within each pane. Warm sun-washed cathedral lighting from upper left on the stone arch frame only. Colors permitted: cream-armor, sandstone-tan, terracotta-orange, deep-jaw-shadow, dim-red-orange-throat-glow, ivory-fang-bone, hollow-eye-socket-shadow, cracked-gold fracture-lines, warm-amber gild-glow, lapis-lazuli-sky, dusk-rose-haze, dark-leading-outline. No friendly bead-eyes, no curious head-tilt. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel itself filling the square canvas.
