## Prompt

Stained glass cathedral window illustration, square 1:1 format, in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style: gothic pointed arch frame with leaded stone pillars left and right entwined with pale-aqua kelp ribbons and tiny glass-panel coral sprigs, trefoil quatrefoil cornices in the arch spandrels, plain leaded inner border (regular-enemy treatment). Subject: the Maren Warrior — a finned tide-folk spear-guard standing centered in the arch, filling about sixty-five percent of the arch height in a poised, guarding stance. Design it from first principles as a lean amphibious humanoid of the drowned reef: a slim hard-muscled figure with smooth overlapping turquoise scales tiling down its shoulders and arms, a fan of stiff membranous fins flaring back from the sides of its jaw like a frilled crest, gill-slits ridging the neck, and webbed three-fingered hands. It grips a slender bone-and-coral war-spear held diagonally across the body, the haft bound with cord and a pale-coral leaf-blade tip. Strands of woven shell-and-pearl beadwork cross its chest like a guardsman's harness. Its eyes are calm and round, alert but not feral — no fangs bared. Our gilding motif marks it as Embertide-touched: a few hairline gold fracture-lines creep across the scales of one shoulder and along the spear-haft, with a faint warm amber glimmer at those seams, just enough to show the corruption taking hold without overwhelming the cool aquatic figure. Background: layered teal and aqua stained-glass panels with cobalt depths and a soft moonlit-water shimmer rippling behind the figure. Warm sun-washed cathedral lighting from upper left on the stone arch frame only, keeping the interior cool and aqua-toned. Thick dark leading lines separating segments, painterly tone variation within each pane. Poised guarding pose, spear held mid-stance, centered with quiet vertical symmetry. Colors permitted: turquoise scale, deep-cobalt-teal shadow, soft-coral fin-and-spear accent, pale-pearl beadwork, cracked-gold hairline fracture-lines, faint-amber seam-glimmer, teal-and-aqua background, cobalt depth, moonlit-water shimmer, dark-leading-outline. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel filling the square canvas.
