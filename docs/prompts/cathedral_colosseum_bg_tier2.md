## Prompt

Stained glass landscape panel, 21:9 panoramic format, Elysian Cathedral card-art style: a covered Umbra colosseum interior at dusk, seen through a leaded-glass window. A wider weathered stone-tile arena floor stretches across the foreground beneath a tall vaulted canopy of ribbed stone arches overhead. Heavy stone walls flank both sides of the arena, hung with long Elysian and Umbra banners (Embertide sigil and Umbra-eye glyph, faded red and faded indigo cloth) draped between the columns. Two iron braziers on tall stone plinths frame the middle distance, their warm flames casting torchlight glow across the walls and a soft amber haze in the air. Beyond the rear arch a dimming dusk sky in deep purple and ember orange shows through, with the silhouette of a single distant Umbra obelisk on the horizon. Ember orange and deep wood-brown dominant with indigo and faded-red banner accents and a torchlit warm-amber haze in the middle ground. Thick dark leading lines separating glass segments, painterly stained-glass tone variation within each segment. Background reads as the second tier of the colosseum — earned-respect, knight-honor stakes, serious-but-warm. Composition is intentionally low-density in the center: the arena floor is mostly empty so that boss-card portraits placed on top remain the visual hero. No monsters, no characters, no figures of any kind in the panel. No text, no letters, no captions, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel itself filling the 21:9 canvas.
