## Prompt

Stained glass cathedral window illustration, square 1:1 format, in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style: gothic pointed arch frame with leaded stone pillars in sun-washed marble, trefoil quatrefoil cornices in the arch spandrels, plain leaded inner border (regular-enemy treatment). Subject: the Gulpmaw — a squat upright swallowing-creature standing centered in the arch, filling about sixty percent of the arch height, wider than it is tall. Design it from first principles as a stout vertical column of slick ringed flesh, banded like a stack of accordion-folds of damp leather, balanced on a short blunt base that grips the marble floor. Its whole top is one enormous gaping circular maw that dominates the body — a deep dark throat ringed with three concentric rows of small inward-curving teeth, like a lamprey's mouth turned to face the ceiling. It has no eyes and no separate head; the maw IS its face. Caught mid-swallow, a single small object — a smooth round amber-glass bead, like a stolen ember sealed in glass — tumbles down into the dark throat, tiny gold sparks trailing behind it. Our gilding motif marks it as ember-tainted: hairline gold fracture-lines run along the ridge-bands of its flesh and a warm amber glow rises up out of the throat from the swallowed light, the tooth-rim catching that glow as a thin line of gold, faint pale-gold vapor curling from the maw's edge. Background: stained-glass panels in sun-gold and amber arranged as radiating light-rays, the warm bright frame contrasting against the dark devouring mouth at center. Warm sun-washed cathedral lighting from upper left on the marble arch frame. Thick dark leading lines separating segments, painterly tone variation within each pane. Centered with strong vertical symmetry, the maw dominant. Colors permitted: muddy-ochre ringed-flesh, slick-umber shadow, dark-throat interior, gold tooth-rim, cracked-gold fracture-lines, warm-amber throat-glow, amber-glass bead, pale-gold vapor, sun-gold background, amber-ray accent, dark-leading-outline. No eyes, no limbs, no separate head. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel filling the square canvas.
