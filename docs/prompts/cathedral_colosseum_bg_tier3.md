## Prompt

Stained glass landscape panel, 21:9 panoramic format, Elysian Cathedral card-art style: a half-ruined ancient Umbra stadium under storm-light, seen through a leaded-glass window. A weathered cracked-tile arena floor stretches across the foreground, partly overgrown with thin patches of pale moss along the seams. Tall broken Umbra pillars rise on either side, their stone faces carved with faintly-glowing pale-cyan Umbra glyphs and circuit-like grooves. Behind the arena, a section of the stadium's vaulted stained-glass ceiling has shattered open, leaving a jagged gap exposing a stormy slate-grey sky overhead. Thin pale-cyan ancient-tech arcs of energy lace through the air across the gap, like distant lightning trapped between the broken ribs of the cathedral. Drifting dust motes catch the cool light. A single quiet ankh-like Umbra glyph glows softly on the central floor tile. Cool slate-grey and storm-grey dominant with pale Umbra-cyan accents on the glyphs and energy arcs, and faint pale-jade moss highlights. Thick dark leading lines separating glass segments, painterly stained-glass tone variation within each segment. Background reads as the third tier of the colosseum — mythic, beyond-knights age, ancient-tech intruding on weathered sacred stone. Composition is intentionally low-density in the center: the arena floor is mostly empty so that boss-card portraits placed on top remain the visual hero. No monsters, no characters, no figures of any kind in the panel. No text, no letters, no captions, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel itself filling the 21:9 canvas.
