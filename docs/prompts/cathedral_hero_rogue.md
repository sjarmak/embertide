## Prompt

Stained glass cathedral window illustration, square 1:1 format, in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style: gothic pointed arch frame with carved-marble pillars left and right entwined with ivy reliefs, trefoil quatrefoil cornices in the arch spandrels, hero-tier gold-leaf rose-window glowing in the upper arch. Subject: a supply-hero rogue of Elysia — a lithe shadow-walker recruited to slip past the gilding's reach, standing three-quarters-forward inside the arch at about sixty-five percent of arch height, slightly off-centre for an elegant asymmetric silhouette. A deep hooded CLOAK of midnight-plum and charcoal conceals most of the face — only a sliver of cheek and a single glinting eye catch the light under the hood's edge. The figure wears close dark leathers and a buckled bandolier of slim throwing-knives across the chest; one gloved hand emerges from the cloak gripping a curved DAGGER that catches a thin cool-silver highlight along its edge, the other hand stays hidden in the folds. At the waist a wide belt carries a single faceted AMETHYST set in bronze, glowing as a secondary focal point. Behind the hood, partly eclipsed by it, a thin gold arcane RING ornament hovers like a broken halo — background ornament only, never dominating the hood. Pose: poised and predatory, weight low, ready to step into shadow. Background: stained-glass panels in deep indigo and amethyst arranged as concentric radial wedges centred on the hood, faint rune-etched cells scattered through the upper spandrels, thick dark leading lines separating segments, painterly tone variation within each pane, cool moody interior with the cathedral light kept to the stone frame only. Colors permitted: midnight-plum cloak, charcoal shadow, dark-leather armor, cool-silver dagger-edge, bronze-set amethyst glow, gold rune-ring highlight, deep-indigo background, amethyst accent, single eye-glint, dark-leading-outline. Elegant asymmetric composition with more visual weight on the dagger side, broad cloak cells below the waist with little fussy detail. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel itself filling the square canvas.
