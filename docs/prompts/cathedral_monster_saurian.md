## Prompt

Stained glass cathedral window illustration, square 1:1 format, in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style: gothic pointed arch frame with leaded stone pillars left and right blackened by soot and veined with glowing ember-red cracks, trefoil quatrefoil cornices in the arch spandrels, plain leaded inner border (regular-enemy treatment). Subject: the Saurian — a lean scaled lizard-humanoid warrior centered in the arch at about sixty-five percent of arch height, standing upright mid-stride in an aggressive lunging pose, a long whip-tail curling behind for balance. Its narrow crocodilian snout is open in a hiss showing a row of sharp white teeth, slit amber eyes glaring forward, a low spined crest running back over the skull. Strapped across its chest and shins are plates of volcanic obsidian; in both clawed hands it brandishes overhead a curved cast-bronze blade. Our gilding motif creeps over the figure: hairline gold fracture-lines branch across the dark scales and the obsidian plates with a faint warm-amber glow leaking from the deepest cracks, the corruption brightest along the spine-crest as though the heat of Emberpeak is gilding the creature alive. Background: molten-crimson and deep-amber stained-glass panels with radiating heat-shimmer rays and a distant black volcanic silhouette on the horizon, thick dark leading lines separating each segment, painterly tone variation within each pane. Warm sun-washed cathedral lighting from upper left on the stone arch frame only, the interior kept hot-toned. Colors permitted: charcoal-grey scale, crimson scale-highlight, obsidian-black armor, cast-bronze blade, cracked-gold fracture-lines, warm-amber gild-glow, slit-amber eye, molten-crimson background, deep-amber heat-haze, dark-leading-outline. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel itself filling the square canvas.
