Tightly framed with no background scenery, no landscape, no sky. The keyhole itself sits at the lava pool's center (circular top with vertical slot below), cut cleanly into a raised blackened-stone lip, with the hot molten glow pouring out of the keyhole opening. A thin gold filigree tracery runs along the outer arch, glowing faintly red-hot. The door is IMPASSABLY SEALED — no light leaks through, no interior visible. Style: painterly stained-glass illustration with thick dark leading outlines separating each stone block, iron band, and the magma-pool edge. Dramatic directional cathedral lighting from upper left catching the glossy black obsidian surface; molten orange underglow in the lava-cracks and in the magma pool + keyhole. Colors permitted: obsidian-black, dark basalt-grey, ember-orange, molten-red, molten-yellow-lava-core, iron-black, deep-charcoal, gold-filigree-accent, red-hot-glow-orange, cooling-crust-black, dark-leading-outline. ABSOLUTELY NO green anywhere on the door, arch, magma pool, or ornament; green is ONLY the flat background behind the subject. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no cathedral pews, no landscape — ONLY the closed boss door with its organic molten-magma keyhole centerpiece, on a pure solid uniform #00FF00 green backdrop.
