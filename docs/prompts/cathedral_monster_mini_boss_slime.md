## Prompt

Stained glass cathedral window illustration, square 1:1 format, in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style: gothic pointed arch frame with leaded stone pillars left and right carved with rippling water-wave reliefs, trefoil quatrefoil cornices in the arch spandrels and gold-leaf filigree, extra ornamental flourish with denser leading (mini-boss treatment). Subject: the Tidewraith — a ghostly water-elemental parasite hovering at the center of the arch, filling about seventy percent of the arch height. Design it from first principles as a body of living water given shape by the burning core trapped inside it: at the heart of the creature floats a single bright pulsing nucleus — a roughly spherical molten amber-and-rose core radiating a soft warm glow, with a dark inner seed at its center like a narrow watchful slit. Coalesced loosely around and suspending that core is a churning translucent body of aqua-blue and cyan water, drawn up into a writhing amorphous mass of curling tendrils that reach outward from the center, a few flinging small water-droplets off their tips. The water is the creature's flesh; the glowing core is its captured heart. Our gilding motif binds the design to the world: the warm core-glow is the caged Embertide burning inside the wraith, and thin hairline gold fracture-lines flicker through the water around the nucleus where the ember's heat has gilded the very water, a faint gold shimmer pulsing outward through the translucent body. Background: deep underwater sapphire-blue and teal stained-glass panels with wavering pale-aqua light-ripples radiating outward, suggesting a flooded vault-chamber lair. Cool aquatic cathedral lighting from upper left with rippling reflective highlights on the water-body. Thick dark leading lines separating segments, painterly tone variation within each pane. A bright burning heart inside a cold ghostly water-body, centered and looming. Colors permitted: molten-amber-rose nucleus, glowing-warm-orange, dark-seed-center, translucent-aqua-blue, cyan-water, sapphire-deep-blue, pale-aqua-ripple, cream-water-highlight, cracked-gold hairline fracture-lines, faint-gold shimmer, gold-filigree accent, dark-leading-outline. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel filling the square canvas.
