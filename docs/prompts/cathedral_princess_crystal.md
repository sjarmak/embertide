## Prompt

Illustration of an isolated subject centered on a SOLID PURE BRIGHT GREEN (#00FF00) flat background — no checkerboard, no gradient, no pattern, no texture, just a uniform saturated green chroma-key backdrop that will be post-processed to transparency. Tightly framed with no background scenery, no arch, no frame, no stone, no landscape. Subject: a single tall faceted crystal of solidified Embertide light — vertically oriented, pointed at the top and the bottom, with six clean flat side-facets between the points, the glass tinted warm molten amber-gold shading into pale honey-white at its core. The crystal has ONE continuous outer silhouette: no star-burst spokes, no radiating shards, no extra pointed facets sticking out beyond the main hexagonal body. Sealed inside, visible through the translucent gold glass: Aurelia, Sovereign of Elysia — a young queen standing upright in calm, regal stillness, her warm-gold hair bound back beneath a circlet of interlocking gold rings echoing the Embertide whorl, eyes open and serene, chin level. She is robed in ivory and pale-gold layered vestments with an Embertide-whorl brooch (a small molten-gold spiral) clasped at her breast; her hands rest composed at her waist. She is rendered in soft warm tones (fair skin, gold hair, ivory robe, gold accents), clearly visible through the amber crystal's translucency, suspended as if the light itself froze around her. Thin gold filigree tracery follows ONLY the outer hexagonal edge of the crystal — no filigree radiating outward, no sun rays, no spoke patterns. A subtle diffuse warm-gold glow-halo immediately around the crystal's silhouette, no further than 5% of the canvas outward, soft and small. Style: painterly stained-glass illustration with thick dark leading outlines along the crystal's single outer silhouette and along a small number of internal facet divisions (4-6 internal facet lines max), saturated amber-gold glass tones inside the crystal. Colors permitted: amber, molten-gold, honey-white, ivory, pale-gold, warm-cream, fair-skin, gold-blonde, dark-outline-brown, soft silver. ABSOLUTELY NO green anywhere on the crystal, the sovereign, the outlines, or the halo — green is ONLY the flat chroma-key background behind the subject. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no stone arch, no cathedral frame, no landscape, no radiating points, no star burst — ONLY a single tall faceted amber-gold Embertide crystal with the sealed sovereign upright inside it, on a pure solid uniform #00FF00 green backdrop.
