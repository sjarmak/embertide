## Prompt

Stained glass landscape panel, 21:9 panoramic format, Elysian Cathedral card-art style: a humble open-air Umbra training colosseum at dawn, seen through a leaded-glass window. A weathered sandstone-tile arena floor stretches across the foreground, low circular Umbra stone pillars ring the arena edges (waist-high, no figures), and a simple stone parapet wall arcs across the middle ground. Beyond the parapet, distant rolling green Elysian hills under a soft dawn-pink and pale-amber sky, with thin wisps of morning mist drifting low. A single subtle Umbra-eye glyph carved faintly into the central floor tile, weathered and partly worn — a quiet hint of the order that built this place. Warm cream and soft gold dominant with dawn-pink mid-tones and pale-jade green accents on the distant hills. Thick dark leading lines separating glass segments, painterly stained-glass tone variation within each segment. Background reads as the entry tier of the colosseum — humble, hopeful, training-ground stakes. Composition is intentionally low-density in the center: the arena floor and middle parapet are mostly empty so that boss-card portraits placed on top remain the visual hero. No monsters, no characters, no figures of any kind in the panel. No text, no letters, no captions, no UI overlays — just the stained-glass panel itself filling the 21:9 canvas.
