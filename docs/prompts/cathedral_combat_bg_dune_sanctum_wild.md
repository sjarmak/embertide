## Prompt

Stained glass landscape panel, 16:9 widescreen format, Elysian Cathedral card-art style: a sun-bleached half-buried Duneborn vault opened to the sky — a sand-choked open ruin courtyard ringed by weathered sandstone pillars leaning slightly inward, the lower courses swallowed by drifted dunes, with a faint armored-sentinel silhouette far in the background haze (a tall metal-clad figure shape in the distant dusk-rose haze, anchoring the wild-boss identity for the iron-sentinel fight). Warm dusk-rose and sandstone-tan sunshafts angle down from the upper left through broken arches, pooling softly on a sand-swept stone floor at lower-center. Drifting sand veils trail horizontally across the mid-band; small dust-motes and warm-amber sparkle drift through the sunshafts. Foreground: weathered flagstone-and-sand floor with a few cracked sandstone shards and faint Duneborn-spiral inscriptions worn nearly smooth at the outer edges. Rendered as a full stained-glass landscape panel: thick dark leading lines segmenting the glass into distinct painterly panes (sky-glass, pillar-glass, dune-glass, floor-glass, each its own leaded region), painterly stained-glass tone variation within each segment, saturated vivid cathedral-glass colors throughout — NOT a muted painterly interior, but a vibrant stained-glass desert ruin come to life in widescreen. Crucially, the lower-center area of the panel is a CLEAR OPEN STAGE — the glass tone at lower-center is intentionally lighter and cleaner (the brightest pooled sand-swept floor), with no creatures, no figures, no text, no UI, no cards, no banners — it reads as a battlefield surface that the UI will sit on top of. The peripheral pillars, upper arches, dune-drifts, and outer corners are noticeably darker than the center stage to frame the eye inward. Overall brightness is dimmed to the 30-40% band (dusk inside a stained-glass desert vault). Colors permitted: warm sandstone-tan, dusk-rose, terracotta-orange, sun-bleached cream, lapis-lazuli accent blue, dusty-amber haze, gold-filigree, soft-ivory highlight, dark-leading-outline. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays, no hard UI edges — just the stained-glass panel itself filling the 16:9 canvas.
