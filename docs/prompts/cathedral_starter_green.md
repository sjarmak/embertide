# cathedral_starter_green

- **Bead:** embertide-rkbf (rename gem → shard + Aurelia-canon vertical-hex art)
- **Card:** starter-green-shard (5x in every 10-card starter deck; +1 green per play)
- **Model:** fal-ai/nano-banana-pro
- **Params:** `aspect_ratio=1:1`, `output_format=png`, `resolution=2K`, `num_images=1`
- **Raster:** `public/illustrations/cathedral_starter_green_001.png`
- **Spec:** `src/illustration/examples/cathedral_starter_green.json`
- **Post-process:** `node scripts/magenta-key-to-alpha.mjs <raster>` — strips the magenta backdrop to alpha so the gem floats on the card chrome's parchment.

## Prompt

Illustration of an isolated subject centered on a SOLID PURE BRIGHT MAGENTA (#FF00FF) flat background — no checkerboard, no gradient, no pattern, no texture, just a uniform fully-saturated magenta backdrop that will be post-processed to transparency. NO cathedral arch, NO stone pillars, NO trefoil cornices, NO architectural frame, NO scenic backdrop, NO rays-of-light, NO gold-filigree border, NO parchment background, NO cream paper — the only thing rendered besides the magenta backdrop is the gem itself. Subject: a single faceted six-sided EMERALD GREEN SHARD rendered with a strictly VERTICAL hexagonal silhouette — point at the very top, point at the very bottom, longer than wide. The gem has TWO horizontal facet seams cutting across the body (one near the top creating a small triangular cap above the upper seam, one near the bottom creating a small triangular cap below the lower seam) PLUS one VERTICAL CENTER SEAM running from the top point all the way down to the bottom point — six visible facet cells total: top-left triangle, top-right triangle, mid-left rectangle, mid-right rectangle, bottom-left triangle, bottom-right triangle. Painterly tonal variation across the six cells: lighter emerald on the upper-left bevel catching cathedral light, mid-tone forest-emerald across the body, deeper emerald on the lower-right bevel in shadow. All interior facet seams are crisp clean dark leaded lines, with subtle gold filigree highlight running along each seam and along the outer six edges. Canonical features: (1) silhouette is UPRIGHT/VERTICAL hexagonal — top edge AND bottom edge each TAPER TO A SHARP POINT, NOT flat-top, NOT flat-bottom, NOT a four-sided diamond, NOT an octagon; height clearly greater than width like a tall narrow upright jewel; (2) interior facet pattern: TWO HORIZONTAL SEAMS plus ONE FULL-HEIGHT VERTICAL CENTER SEAM, dividing the gem into exactly six cells; (3) jewel-cut painterly tonal variation between the cells; (4) thick dark leaded outlines on the six outer edges and on every interior seam, with subtle warm gold filigree highlights tracing the leading; (5) a small ivory-white light-catch sliver in the upper-left of the top cap suggesting reflected window light; (6) the gem occupies roughly seventy percent of the canvas height, centered, perfectly upright. Style: stylized stained-glass card-art aesthetic with thick dark leading and painterly gradients within each cell — NOT flat cel-shading, NOT noisy textures. Color palette: forest-green and emerald-jewel tones for the gem only; everything outside the gem is solid uniform pure magenta #FF00FF. The MAGENTA BACKDROP IS REQUIRED — do NOT render a parchment, cream, or beige background; the background MUST be pure saturated magenta so it can be alpha-keyed. ABSOLUTELY NO magenta, pink, fuchsia, or purple anywhere on the gem itself — the gem is exclusively forest-green / emerald tones with gold leading and ivory highlight; magenta is reserved for the flat backdrop only. Friendly kid-6yo storybook tone (a treasure-jewel that feels rewarding to collect). Explicitly DO NOT render: a cathedral arch frame, stained-glass window panels around the gem, gothic architecture, stone pillars, trefoil cornices, rose-window backdrop, gold filigree borders around the canvas, radiating light rays, halos, multiple gems, a coin, a ring, a four-sided diamond/kite, a horizontal hexagon with flat top and flat bottom (that is the WRONG silhouette — top and bottom MUST be sharp points), an octagon, a circle, a square, a teardrop, a heart-cut gem, a round cabochon, any human figure, any creature, any sword or shield, any background scenery, any cream/parchment background, gritty/realistic textures, photo-realistic noise, hyper-detailing, harsh cel-shading. The shard MUST be a single upright vertical hexagonal gem with point top and point bottom, longer than wide, with two horizontal facet seams AND a vertical center seam dividing it into six cells. The background MUST be solid pure uniform #FF00FF magenta everywhere outside the gem silhouette. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no UI overlays, no banners, no ribbons, no scrolls, no runes, no inscriptions of any kind — just the isolated stained-glass green shard centered on a uniform magenta backdrop.
