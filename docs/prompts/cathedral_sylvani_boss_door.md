## Prompt

Illustration of an isolated subject centered on a SOLID PURE BRIGHT GREEN (#00FF00) flat background — no checkerboard, no gradient, no pattern, no texture, just a uniform saturated green chroma-key backdrop that will be post-processed to transparency. Tightly framed with no background scenery, no landscape, no sky. Subject: a massive closed forest-temple DOUBLE DOOR in the Elysian Cathedral card-art style — arched gothic doorway filling ~90% of the square canvas, facing the viewer straight-on and CLOSED (no gap between the two door leaves). The door is built from rough-hewn ancient oak planks bound with dark bronze iron straps and crossbeams; heavy iron hinges along the outer edges, bronze studs along the planks. The stone arch frame around the door is weathered pale grey limestone entangled with thick leafy green ivy vines, mossy patches, small wooden thornkin-sprout motifs carved into the keystone, and fern fronds curling from the corners. At the dead-center meeting point of the two door leaves, a LARGE prominent boss-key keyhole: an ornate carved wooden escutcheon plate shaped like a stylized thornkin leaf, with a keyhole silhouette at its center glowing faint warm amber from within. The keyhole shape is clearly readable as a key slot (circular top, vertical slot below). A thin gold filigree tracery runs along the outer arch and around the escutcheon. The door is IMPASSABLY SEALED — no light leaks through, no interior visible. Style: painterly stained-glass illustration with thick dark leading outlines separating each plank, stone block, ivy clump, and the escutcheon. Warm directional cathedral lighting from upper left catching the raised grain of the wood and the leaves of the ivy; cool shadow in the keyhole and in the crevices between planks. Colors permitted: oak-brown, deep walnut, bronze, iron-grey, pale-limestone-grey, forest-green, moss-green, gold-filigree-accent, warm-amber-keyhole-glow, dark-leading-outline. ABSOLUTELY NO green anywhere on the door, arch, ivy, or ornament — the ivy foliage uses forest-green / moss-green tones that are distinctly darker and more desaturated than pure chroma-key #00FF00; green is ONLY the flat background behind the subject. No text, no letters, no captions, no watermarks, no cathedral pews, no landscape — ONLY the closed boss door with its keyhole centerpiece, on a pure solid uniform #00FF00 green backdrop.
